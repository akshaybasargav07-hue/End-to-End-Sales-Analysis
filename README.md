Sales Analysis Dashboard Project:

🔹Project Overview:
This project analyzes sales data to generate business insights using SQL, Python, and Power BI. The goal is to support data-driven decision-making by identifying trends in sales, customer behavior, and product performance.

🔹 Tools & Technologies:
1.Python (Pandas)
2.SQL Server
3.Power BI
4.Excel

🔹 Dataset:
1.Rows: 1000
2.Columns: 14
3.Includes product, sales, customer, and transaction data

🔹 Key Analysis Performed:
1.Total Sales by Region
2.Top Products by Sales
3.Sales by Sales Representative
4.Monthly Sales Trend
5.Profit Analysis
6.Payment Method Analysis

🔹 Dashboard Features:
1.KPI Cards (Sales, Profit, Quantity)
2.Sales Trend (Time-based)
3.Region-wise Performance
4.Product Category Analysis
5.Customer Segmentation

🔹 Business Insights:
1.High-performing regions drive most revenue
2.Discount impacts profit margins
3.Certain sales reps outperform others
4.Sales channel and payment method influence customer behavior

🔹 Business Recommendations:
1.Focus on high-performing regions
2.Optimize discount strategies
3.Promote high-margin products
4.Improve low-performing regions
5.Reward top sales representatives
